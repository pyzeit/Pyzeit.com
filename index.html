Straight Answers

Questions We Get Asked

What does "partner" mean for Foundation services?+
For company registration, GST, contracts, and licenses — we work with qualified CAs and legal professionals we've vetted. You still deal only with Pyzeit: we coordinate, manage quality, and handle all communication.
Why only specific services instead of more?+
We'd rather deliver our core services with real depth than list 30 and deliver them superficially. As we grow our team, we'll add services — but only when we can do them properly.
How is Pyzeit different from a CA firm + a design agency?+
A CA firm handles compliance. A design agency handles visuals. Neither talks to other. At Pyzeit, the person filing your GST understands your brand, and your developer has reviewed your legal structure. That cross-awareness eliminates mistakes.
How quickly can my project start?+
Typically within 3-5 business days. Some services like company registration can begin within 24 hours.
I'm based outside India. Can you still help?+
Yes, absolutely. Our founder completed his MS in Germany and holds strong connections with European Union, making us uniquely equipped to assist global businesses.

Our name "Pyzeit" itself reflects our philosophy: "Py" for Technology and "Zeit" (German for Time). We believe in adopting technology to save you time and deliver premium service to all clients, whether you are in Europe, US, or anywhere else in the world.
Are there long-term contracts?+
No. Every engagement is project-based with defined deliverables and costs.
What if I only need one service — like just a logo?+
That's completely fine. Many clients start with a single need and expand over time.
